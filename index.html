our values

JomJual is built on trust, transparency, and community. Every transaction connects a student seller with a fellow student buyer right on campus — safe, fast, and easy.

about us

We're UiTM students building a marketplace for UiTM students. Whether you're selling homemade food, preloved clothes, or handmade crafts — JomJual is your campus store.

our mission

To empower campus entrepreneurs and help students find what they need without leaving their dorm. Supporting local sellers, one hostel block at a time.
